# Project overview

The Barstol.bg project involved the creation of a new self-hosted e-commerce website for Barstol, a local provider of
chairs. The website aimed to provide an online platform for customers to explore and purchase a wide range of chairs.
This project was built using various web development technologies, including WordPress, PHP, MySQL, JavaScript, CSS,
Bootstrap, and WooCommerce.

# Key contributions

- Collaborated closely with the client, Barstol, to understand their specific e-commerce needs and requirements.
- Designed and developed a user-friendly and visually appealing e-commerce website with an intuitive layout.
- Leveraged WordPress as the content management system and integrated WooCommerce to facilitate the online shopping
  experience.
- Implemented custom features and functionalities to categorize and showcase a diverse range of chairs.
- Utilized JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, CSS, and Bootstrap to create a responsive and performance-optimized website.
- Ensured the highest standards of security for online transactions and user data protection.

# Project impact

Barstol.bg served as a valuable online sales platform for Barstol, enabling them to reach a wider customer base and
streamline their e-commerce operations. The project demonstrated my ability to create effective e-commerce solutions
that meet the unique needs of local businesses and enhance their online presence.
