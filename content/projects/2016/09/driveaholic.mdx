# Project overview

Driveaholic was a personal project born out of a passion for cars and automotive content. This project was a creative
experiment in the form of an auto blog. The development of this auto blog involved utilizing a variety of web
technologies, including WordPress, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, CSS, and Bootstrap.

# Key highlights

- Driven by a personal passion for cars and automotive content.
- Created as an experimental auto blog to share insights, experiences, and stories related to automobiles.
- Leveraged WordPress, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, CSS, and Bootstrap to build and manage the blog.
- Demonstrated the power of web technologies in pursuing personal interests and hobbies.

# Project impact

Driveaholic served as a platform to express a deep-rooted passion for automobiles. While not designed for commercial
purposes, this project highlighted the ability of web technologies to empower individuals to share their interests and
experiences with a global audience. It showcased the versatility of web development for personal pursuits.
