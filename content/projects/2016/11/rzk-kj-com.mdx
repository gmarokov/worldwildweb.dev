# Project overview

The RZK-KJ.com project involved the development of a website for a local craft house in Kardzhali. The website aimed to
showcase the craft house's unique products, provide information about their services, and offer a platform for promoting
their craftwork. The project made extensive use of various web development technologies, including WordPress, PHP,
MySQL, JavaScript, CSS, and Bootstrap.

# Key contributions

- Worked closely with the local craft house to understand their specific requirements and objectives.
- Designed a visually appealing and user-friendly website structure that effectively highlighted the craft house's
  products and services.
- Implemented WordPress as the content management system to enable easy content updates and management by the client.
- Developed custom features and functionalities to showcase the craft house's craftwork and promote their business.
- Utilized JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, CSS, and Bootstrap to ensure a responsive and visually engaging website.
- Ensured that the website met high standards of performance, security, and responsive design.

# Project impact

The RZK-KJ.com website played a crucial role in establishing an online presence for the local craft house in Kardzhali.
It provided the craft house with a valuable platform to showcase their products, attract customers, and promote their
unique craftwork. The project demonstrated my proficiency in web development and my commitment to delivering solutions
that align with the client's vision.

View the website at [RZK-KJ.com](https://rzk-kj.com/)
