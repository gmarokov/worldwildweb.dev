# Project overview

The Kardjali-bg.com project involved the creation of a website aimed at providing news and updates from the business
community in the region of Kardzhali. The website was designed to serve as a valuable source of information for both
residents and businesses in the region. The project leveraged various web development technologies, including WordPress,
PHP, MySQL, JavaScript, CSS, and Bootstrap.

# Key contributions

- Collaborated closely with local businesses and organizations to gather news and updates relevant to the Kardzhali
  region.
- Designed an intuitive and user-friendly website layout that ensured easy access to news and information.
- Implemented WordPress as the content management system to facilitate efficient content management and updates.
- Developed custom functionalities and features to categorize and display news, events, and updates.
- Employed JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, CSS, and Bootstrap to create a responsive and visually appealing website.
- Ensured that the website met high standards of performance, security, and responsive design.

# Project impact

Kardjali-bg.com served as a vital online platform for sharing news and updates within the Kardzhali business community.
The project contributed to fostering connections and providing valuable information to local residents and businesses.
It demonstrated my expertise in web development and my dedication to delivering solutions tailored to the needs of the
local community.

View the website at [Kardjali-bg.com](https://kardjali-bg.com/)
