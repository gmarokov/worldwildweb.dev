# Project overview

Procitizen is an ASP.NET MVC web application designed to facilitate the reporting of issues by citizens to their local
municipalities. This project addresses the vital need for citizens to engage with their local governments by providing a
platform to voice concerns, report problems, and contribute to the improvement of their communities.

# Key highlights

- Utilizes ASP.NET technology, JavaScript, and Razor for the development of a robust web application.
- Built with the goal of enhancing civic engagement by simplifying the process of issue reporting.
- Seamlessly connects citizens with local municipalities to promote community improvement.
- Emphasizes open-source collaboration through a public repository accessible at:
  [Procitizen repository](https://github.com/gmarokov/Procitizen).

# Project impact

Procitizen serves as a valuable tool to bridge the gap between citizens and their local government. By providing a
user-friendly platform for issue reporting, it empowers individuals to actively participate in the betterment of their
communities. The open-source nature of the project encourages collaboration and improvements in the realm of civic
engagement.

This project has the potential to make a significant difference in local communities by fostering a sense of
responsibility, participation, and shared commitment to civic betterment.
