# Project overview

I had the honor of working on the creation of Daniel Delchev's personal website, https://Daniel-Delchev.com. This
project was designed to serve as a portfolio showcasing his work and achievements. It involved the use of various web
development technologies, including WordPress, PHP, MySQL, JavaScript, CSS, and Bootstrap.

# Key contributions

- Collaborated closely with Daniel Delchev to understand his vision and objectives for the website.
- Utilized WordPress as the content management system to provide an easy-to-update and manage solution.
- Developed custom functionality and design to align with Daniel's personal brand and style.
- Integrated JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, CSS, and Bootstrap to create a responsive and visually appealing website.
- Ensured the website's performance and functionality met the client's expectations.

# Project impact

The website successfully presented Daniel Delchev's portfolio and personal brand to the world. It provided an effective
platform for showcasing his work, achievements, and professional background. The project demonstrated my proficiency in
web development and the ability to translate a client's vision into a functional and visually appealing website.

View the website at [Daniel-Delchev.com](https://daniel-delchev.com)
