# Project overview

The Incubator-kj.com project involved the development of a website for a local business incubator in Kardzhali. The
website aimed to serve as a digital platform for the incubator to showcase its services, provide resources to
entrepreneurs, and promote local business growth. The project utilized various web development technologies, including
WordPress, PHP, MySQL, JavaScript, CSS, and Bootstrap.

# Key contributions

- Collaborated closely with the local business incubator to understand their specific needs and objectives.
- Designed a user-friendly and informative website structure that highlighted the incubator's services and offerings.
- Implemented WordPress as the content management system, enabling easy content updates and management by the client.
- Developed custom features and functionalities to support entrepreneurs and the local business community.
- Utilized JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, CSS, and Bootstrap to create a responsive and visually appealing website.
- Ensured that the website met high standards of performance, security, and accessibility.

# Project impact

The Incubator-kj.com website played a pivotal role in establishing a strong online presence for the local business
incubator in Kardzhali. It provided entrepreneurs and businesses in the region with a valuable resource for information,
support, and networking. The project demonstrated my expertise in web development and my dedication to delivering
solutions that align with the client's vision.

View the website at [Incubator-kj.com](https://incubator-bg.com)
