I'm Georgi Marokov, a software engineer. I specialize in .NET, operations, and a bit of front-end technologies. By day,
I tackle complex challenges with elegant solutions. When I'm not working, I cherish moments with loved ones, biking,
hiking, and capturing pictures.

## Then

In the early days of computers, I was fascinated. We'd hack games, create hobby websites, and even launch multiple
successful game servers. Later on, I've started deep diving into website development in my free time, dreaming of
starting my own business (though it didn't quite work out!). Grouping with a few friends, we even had our own website
development studio. Shortly after, I landed my first IT job, starting as a technical support specialist and later
transitioning to a quality assurance role. During my time there, I also pursued C# development at a newly established
software development university.

## Now

Today I'm software engineer working at @Modeshift. Together we build the software of the future public transportation.
Here, you'll find a bit of everything - cloud-native applications based on .NET, mobile development with React Native,
and a lot of IoT devices..

[Find some of my latest technology insights and monologues](/blogs). These days I write mostly about .NET, JavaScript
and DevOps activities.

My passion has evolved to creating innovative experiences, including static web apps, serverless architecture, and
cloud-native apps. [You may want to check out my projects here.](/projects).

Dedicated section for my hobbies at [Trail Tales](/hobbies) where I'll be sharing product reviews, trail experiences,
and pictures from some of the most epic adventures.
